Dataset License
===============

The Humanize datasets consist of short, human-written text snippets used solely to extract structural writing patterns.

License
-------

All dataset files included in this repository are licensed under **Creative Commons Zero (CC0)** unless otherwise stated.

This means:
- The data is free to use, modify, and redistribute
- No attribution is required
- The data may be used for any purpose

Contributor assurances
----------------------

By contributing text to the dataset, you confirm that:
- You have the legal right to share the text
- The text is not proprietary or confidential
- The text is not scraped from restricted sources
- You are comfortable releasing it under CC0

Intent
------

The dataset is not intended to reproduce content or style of any individual author.
It exists to capture broad structural patterns in human writing such as rhythm, sentence length, and punctuation usage.

Removal requests
----------------

If you are a contributor and wish to remove your submitted data, you may request removal at any time by opening an issue.

Requests will be honored without question.
